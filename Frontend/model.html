Machine Learning Model

The Credible system uses Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze news articles and detect misinformation. The platform integrates the Google Gemini AI model to evaluate the credibility of news content.

AI Model Used

Credible uses the Google Gemini AI model to analyze news text and determine whether the information is credible, misleading, or false. The model evaluates claims within the news article and identifies potential misinformation.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Natural Language Processing allows the system to understand and interpret human language. The model analyzes sentence structure, keywords, and context to evaluate the credibility of the information provided.

Analysis Workflow

  • User enters a news article or claim
  • The frontend sends the request to the Flask backend
  • The backend forwards the text to the Gemini AI model
  • The AI analyzes the credibility of the claim
  • The result is returned to the user interface

Model Output

  • Verdict: True, False, or Misleading
  • Confidence Score: Percentage showing model certainty
  • Explanation: Reason for the classification

Benefits of AI Fact Checking

  • Helps detect misinformation quickly
  • Automates credibility analysis of news
  • Supports users in verifying online content
  • Promotes responsible news consumption
